Who is Ichigo Kurosaki?

Ichigo Kurosaki (黒崎 一護, Kurosaki Ichigo) is a Human who is also a Substitute Soul Reaper. Ichigo is the son of Isshin and Masaki Kurosaki, and older brother of Karin and Yuzu.

About Bleach

Facts

It began broadcasting in Japan on October 5, 2004, on TV Tokyo. It ceased broadcasting on March 27, 2012; a total of 366 episodes were aired before the anime concluded. However, it was announced on March 20th 2020 that the final arc of the manga would finally be animated and will begin airing in October 2022.
